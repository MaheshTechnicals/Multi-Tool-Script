What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is scrambled Latin text derived from sections 1.10.32 and 1.10.33 of "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" (The Extremes of Good and Evil) by Cicero, written in 45 BC. This classical Latin literature has been used as placeholder text since the 1960s and remains the industry standard for design and typesetting.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Lorem Ipsum mean?

Lorem Ipsum is scrambled Latin text that doesn't have a direct translation. It's derived from classical Latin literature but has been altered to be nonsensical, making it perfect as placeholder text.

Why not just use "test text" or random words?

Lorem Ipsum has a natural distribution of letters and word lengths that closely resembles real text, making it ideal for testing layouts and typography. Random words or repeated phrases don't provide realistic text flow.

How much Lorem Ipsum should I use?

The amount depends on your project. For web pages, 2-3 paragraphs are typical. For headlines, 5-10 words work well. For body content, 50-200 words per section is common.

Is it okay to use Lorem Ipsum in client presentations?

Yes, Lorem Ipsum is widely recognized as professional placeholder text. It helps clients focus on design and layout rather than getting distracted by content that isn't final.
