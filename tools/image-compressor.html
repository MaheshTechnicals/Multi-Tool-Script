Professional Tips for Image Compression

Quality Settings Guide

90-100%: Minimal compression, best for print or archival purposes
70-90%: Excellent quality for web use, good balance of size and quality
50-70%: Good quality for thumbnails and preview images
Below 50%: High compression for very small file sizes

Best Practices

• Always keep a backup of your original images
• Test different quality settings to find the optimal balance
• Consider your use case: web, print, or storage
• Use WebP format for modern browsers when possible
