Professional Image Format Converter

Transform your images between different formats effortlessly with our advanced format converter. Whether you need to convert JPG to PNG for transparency, optimize images with WebP, or create GIFs for animations, our tool handles all major image formats with precision and quality control.

Supported Image Formats

JPEG/JPG

Perfect for photographs and images with many colors. Offers excellent compression with adjustable quality settings.

PNG

Ideal for images requiring transparency, logos, and graphics with sharp edges. Lossless compression maintains perfect quality.

WebP

Modern format offering superior compression and quality. Perfect for web optimization and faster loading times.

GIF

Classic format supporting animations and transparency. Great for simple graphics and animated content.

How to Convert Image Formats

  1. Upload Your Image: Drag and drop your image file or click to browse. Supports files up to 10MB.

  4. Convert & Preview: Click convert to process your image and preview the results side-by-side.

When to Use Different Formats

Use JPG When:

  • Working with photographs
  • Need smaller file sizes
  • Transparency not required
  • Sharing on social media

Use PNG When:

  • Need transparent backgrounds
  • Working with logos or graphics
  • Require lossless quality
  • Sharp edges and text

Use WebP When:

  • Optimizing for web performance
  • Need modern compression
  • Want smaller file sizes
  • Supporting modern browsers

Use GIF When:

  • Creating simple animations
  • Need wide compatibility
  • Working with limited colors
  • Sharing animated content

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the maximum file size I can convert?

Our converter supports images up to 10MB in size. This covers most use cases while ensuring fast processing times.

Will converting reduce image quality?

Quality depends on the format and settings. PNG conversion is lossless, while JPG and WebP allow quality adjustment. Our tool preserves maximum quality by default.

Can I convert animated GIFs to other formats?

Yes, but only the first frame will be converted to static formats like JPG or PNG. To maintain animation, keep the GIF format or convert to WebP.

Is my data safe when using this converter?

Absolutely! All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.
